Objective:

    To correctly guess the number within the range of 1 to X, depending on the chosen difficulty level, in the least amount of attempts possible and make it to the top of the scoreboard.

Rules/Instructions:

  1. Choose a difficulty level
  2. Enter a number you think the game has chosen
  3. The game will give you hints on whether your guess is too low or too high
  4. You're number of attempts will be shown below
  5. The numbers you have previously entered will be shown below as well
  6. Once you guess the number right, enter your name for it to be placed on the scoreboard
  7. The person with the lowest attempts of guessing the number right wins!
  8. To start a new game press the "New Game" button
  9. To play music, click the music note on the top right, to stop playing music, double click the music note
